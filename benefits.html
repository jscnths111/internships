THE BENEFITS OF INTERNSHIP FOR HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS

  • Interning builds your skills - regardless of whether you intern in architecture, law, veterinary medicine, or just about any other field, internships allow you to develop professional skills beneficial to your future career. Internships will provide personal and professional growth to help you prepare for working in the real world, and may also help you one day land that dream job.

  • Interning helps you stand out - get noticed and get rewarded for your hard work. By choosing to intern, you will demonstrate that you are proactive and serious about your future and possess the skill set to be successful in their program.I

  • Interning provides you with invaluable resources - be prepared to make industry connections that will last a lifetime. You could earn a glowing letter of reference, as well as receive resume writing support and gain networking skills.

  • Interning gives you hands-on experience - experienced mentors will help you discover and explore your areas of interest and assist you in developing practical, real-world skills.

  • Interning helps refine your career goals - whether you love your internship or discover the industry may not be right for you, an internship offers you the chance to make an informed decision about your college and career plans before committing to a university, field of study, or full-time job.
