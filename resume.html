PARTS OF A RESUME

Contact Information

  • What to include: Include your full name, street address, city, state, and zip code. Also include your phone number and email address. If you have a LinkedIn page or personal website, include these URL in your contact section as well.
  • Name: This should be the name you use on all of your job application documents.
  • Mailing address: Use a permanent mailing address. Be sure to include your apartment number and correct zip code. Verify address format at USPS.com.
  • Email address: Use a personal email address that contains your first and last name.
  • Phone number: It is very important to have voicemail with a professional message that contains your full name. If applicable, indicate (text accepted) next to your phone number.

Objective

  • A resume objective is a statement of your goals for employment, typically listed at the top of your resume. A resume objective is often one or two sentences long.
  • High School student seeking an internship in your organization where I can receive professional, real-world experiences that will assist me in determining my future college and career goals

Experience

  • List the companies you have worked for, dates of employment, the positions you held, and a bulleted list of responsibilities and achievements. Internships, summer jobs, and temporary jobs can be included in this part of your resume.

Education

  • List the schools you have attended, an appropriate GPA, and any certifications, special awards and honors you have earned.
  • In addtion, list the names of any extracurricular clubs, charitable groups, or organizations you have been active in.

Skills

  • Include your abilities that are relevant to the internship position that you are interestd in, such as computer skills, software skills and language skills.
  • Customize the skills section to match, as closely as you can, the requirements listed in the job posting.
